👋 Hi! Record your voice once — your child hears YOU every time!

No app can make perfect phonics sounds — the only way to get it right is a real human voice. Record each sound below (takes about 10 minutes total). The sounds save to this browser permanently.

  1. Tap 🔴 Record — say the phonics sound clearly (NOT the letter name!)
  2. Tap ⏹ Stop when done
  3. Tap ▶ Play to check it sounds right
  4. When all done, tap 💾 Export All Sounds to save a file
  5. Put my-sounds.js in the same folder as the app on GitHub
